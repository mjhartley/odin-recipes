Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Fry the beef and set aside
  2. Chop the onions
  3. Fry the Onions
  4. Add the tomatoes
  5. Add the beef
  6. Season with salt and pepper
